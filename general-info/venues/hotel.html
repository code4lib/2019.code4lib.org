Lodging

The conference has a block of rooms reserved at the DoubleTree, starting at a daily rate of $200 ($199 plus a dollar fee, not including an additional occupancy tax). This rate is valid between the dates of February 18th and the 24th. While the reservation will require a credit card, it will not be charged until you arrive. There will be a reservation URL in your registration confirmation.

Check-in is at 3pm while check-out is noon. Included in our reservation is free access to the hotel's guest WiFi network. Parking has also been negotiated at a substantially reduced rate of $12/day in the hotel lot.

If you are unwilling or unable to stay in the conference hotel, there are numerous other lodging options nearby due to the proximity of the San Jose International Airport. A few of your options are:

  • The Fairfield Inn & Suites
  • Courtyard by Marriott
  • SpringHill Suites by Marriott
  • Motel 6
