Accommodations

The Code4Lib community aims to make the conference accessible for attendees of all abilities. The DoubleTree has a length list of accessible services on its Amenities & Services page including accessible rooms, restaurants, parking, and routes throughout the hotel campus.

Accommodation Requests

When registering, attendees were asked to include any accessibility needs they might require. We are doing our best to meet these needs. Everyone is encouraged to contact the accessibility committee if they have questions or new accommodation needs arise. The accessibility committee may be contacted by:

  • Talking with a volunteer at the registration desk who will forward the request
  • Asking for help from a community support volunteer
